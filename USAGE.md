## First Time Setup (2 minutes)

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Select **Groq** as API provider
3. Paste your API key
4. Click **Save Settings**

## Create Your First Deck (1 minute)

1. Click **New Deck**
2. Name it: "Test Deck"
3. Click **Create Deck**

## Generate Cards (2 minutes)

1. In your Test Deck, paste this sample text:

```
Photosynthesis is the process by which plants use sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide to create oxygen and energy in the form of sugar. The process occurs in chloroplasts, which contain chlorophyll - the green pigment that gives plants their color. There are two main stages: the light-dependent reactions and the Calvin cycle. The light reactions convert light energy into chemical energy (ATP and NADPH), while the Calvin cycle uses that energy to convert CO2 into glucose.
```

2. Set number of cards: **5**
3. Click **Generate Flashcards**
4. Wait 10-20 seconds

## Study Session (3 minutes)

1. Click **Study Now**
2. Listen to the question
3. Think of your answer (10 second pause)
4. Listen to the correct answer
5. Rate yourself:
   - Press **Ctrl+1** if you got it wrong (Again)
   - Press **Ctrl+2** if it was hard
   - Press **Ctrl+3** if you got it right (Good)
   - Press **Ctrl+4** if it was easy
6. Next card plays automatically!

## Tips

### For Best AI Generation:
- Use 500-1500 words of clear text
- Academic or textbook content works best
- Generate 10-20 cards at a time
- Avoid very short snippets (< 200 words)
- Don't use messy/unstructured notes

### For Effective Studying:
- Study daily - even just 5-10 minutes
- Use hotkeys for speed
- Be honest with ratings
- Edit cards that don't make sense
- Study while multitasking (cooking, exercising, etc.)

### For ADHD/Multitaskers:
- Listen mode allows you to study hands-free
- Auto-advance keeps you engaged
- Short 10-second intervals maintain focus
- Hotkeys reduce cognitive load of clicking
- Works great during: walking, cleaning, driving (be careful!), exercising

## Troubleshooting Quick Fixes

**Problem**: "No cards due to study"
- Solution: Wait for cards to be due, or generate more cards

**Problem**: Hotkeys not working
- Solution: Run app as administrator (Windows) or grant permissions (Mac)
- Alternative: Click buttons instead

**Problem**: AI generates weird cards
- Solution: Edit or delete them before studying
- Tip: Provide clearer/more structured source text

**Problem**: "API Error"
- Check API key is correct
- Verify you have API credits remaining
- Try different AI provider
